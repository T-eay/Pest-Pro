About us

Pest control is the process of controlling, managing, minimizing or removing undesirable insects and other pests, from spaces occupied by people. It should: Be handled by a licensed pest professionals such as us.

We specialize in removal and prevention of infestations by insects and rodents, including cockroaches, fleas, ticks, ants, bees, mice, and a host of other irritating invasive species. We offer monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly service intervals, with no long-term contracts.
